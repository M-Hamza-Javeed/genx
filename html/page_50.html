Applying the Unix Philosophy

These philosophical principles aren’t just vague generalities. In the Unix world they come straight

from experience and lead to speciﬁc prescriptions, some of which we’ve already developed above.

Here’s a by no means exhaustive list:

• Everything that can be a source- and destination-independent ﬁlter should be one.

• Data streams should if at all possible be textual (so they can be viewed and ﬁltered with standard

tools).
